About Me

I am a Business Information and Technology student, at St Paul’s University in Nairobi Kenya, currently awaiting graduation. Through this course, I was able to gain interest in web development as I did some practice as part of my coursework.

I am a dedicated, methodical, and organized individual with excellent communication abilities. I am great at developing innovative solutions to problems and advocating for a win-win situation. I'm always keen to learn new things and face new difficulties.

This also translates into my web development journey as the front-end aspect of it triggers my creativity. Therefore, having an opportunity to create something beautiful from scratch gives me joy.
